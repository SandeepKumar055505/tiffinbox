QUESTION: Which Event Bus / Job Queue approach? (free only)
A

pg-boss (PostgreSQL-based)

Jobs stored in Neon DB. Zero new services. Runs inside your Express backend. Free forever. Best fit — you already have PostgreSQL.

✓ Zero infra cost · Zero new service · Survives restarts · Retry + cron built-in
✗ Slightly slower than Redis-based queues
B

Vercel Cron Jobs (native)

Vercel's built-in cron. Calls your API endpoints on a schedule. No separate job queue — just HTTP triggers at set intervals.

✓ Zero setup · Native to Vercel · Free on hobby plan
✗ Min interval 1 min · No job state/retry · Not good for event-driven jobs
C

Upstash QStash (HTTP queue)

Free 500 messages/day HTTP-based queue. Push a message, QStash calls your endpoint. Retry + delay built-in.

✓ Free 500/day · Retry built-in · No server needed
✗ 500/day may be tight · External service dependency
